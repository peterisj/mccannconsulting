McCANN Worldgroup

McCANN Consulting is a division of the group of communication companies McCANN Worldgroup Riga, which consists of the advertising agency McCANN Riga and the audio-video studio McCANN Live. The group also owns shares in companies Inspired Latvia, Inspired Digital and Brand Box.

McCANN Consulting

McCANN Consulting is a consulting company providing communication management. We believe that strategic and systemic communication helps the company to achieve its business targets. We ensure that our clients make and maintain relationships with the main stakeholders – consumers, employees, business partners, public authorities, mass media and others. We are convinced that the process of making such relationships should be based on thorough research and analysis, as well as mutual engagement, trust and transparency.

Our values:

  • Passion
  • Trust
  • Development

Passion

We love what we do. We are not afraid of facing new challenges, moving limits and creating new possibilities for our clients and ourselves. We will always do more than others expect.

Trust

Trust is not granted, it must be earned. We always keep promises and take responsibility for the results of our work. The highest appraisal possible is client’s trust.

Development

To be one step ahead – to foresee what will be important tomorrow, it is the biggest professional challenge of any communication manager. We believe we can do it. To achieve it, we constantly improve our knowledge and skills.
